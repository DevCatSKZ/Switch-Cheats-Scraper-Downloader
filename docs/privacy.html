Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Switch Cheats Scraper & Downloader (“the project”) is a set of open-source tools by DevCatSKZ — a Windows app, a Nintendo Switch homebrew app, an Android app and this website. The project collects no personal data: no analytics, no trackers, no advertising and no user accounts on our side. Below is exactly what the tools connect to, and why.

1. This website

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics or tracking. Your language choice is kept only in your browser’s local storage so the site remembers it on your next visit — you can clear it any time in your browser. As with any website, GitHub’s servers may process your IP address and standard request data to deliver the page.

2. The apps run on your device

The Windows, Switch and Android apps run entirely on your own device. Their data — the cheat database, downloaded files and settings — stays local, and none of it is sent to us. We run no server that receives your data.

3. Storage access

The apps request file/storage access only to write downloaded cheat files into your emulator, SD card or a folder you choose, and to keep their local database. They never read, collect or upload your other files.

4. Network connections

To do their job, the apps connect to GitHub (to download the cheat data and check for updates) and to the public cheat sources (such as CheatSlips and the community archives). These requests download cheats only — nothing about you is sent, beyond the technical data any web request necessarily includes (such as your IP address, which those services can see).

5. CheatSlips credentials

If you enter your own CheatSlips.com login or API token in the Windows tool, it is stored locally on your device and sent only to CheatSlips to authenticate you. We never receive or store it. Use only your own account and respect CheatSlips’ terms of service.

6. Third-party services

The tools rely on GitHub (hosting, releases) and on the cheat sources listed on the site. When you connect to them, their own privacy policies and terms apply. We have no control over, and take no responsibility for, those third-party services.

7. Children

The project is not directed at children and knowingly collects no data from anyone, including children.

8. Your rights

Because we collect and store no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct or delete. If you have questions about your rights (for example under the GDPR), just get in touch and we’ll help.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the tools change. The current version always lives at this URL, with its “last updated” date at the top.

10. Contact

Responsible for this project: DevCatSKZ. Questions about privacy? Email devcatskz@gmail.com.
